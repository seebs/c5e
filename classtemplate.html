Class Features

As a xyzzy, you have the following class features.

Hit Points

Hit Dice: 1dX per xyzzy level.

Hit Points at 1st Level: X + your Constitution modifier

Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1dX (or half round up) + your Constitution modifier per xyzzy level after 1st

Proficiencies

Armor: Pick some

Weapoons: A sharp wit

Tools: Something.

Saving Throws: Pick two, usually one dex/wis/con, one str/int/cha

Skills: Pick (two to four) from (a longer list)

Equipment

You start with the following equipment:

  • something useful.
  • (a)something else useful or (b) a different thing.
  • a ring of three wishes.

Archetype or Path Selection

At third level you pick one of the path/archetype options.

Ability Score Improvement

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score by 2, or you can increase two ability scores by 1, in either case to a maximum of 20.
